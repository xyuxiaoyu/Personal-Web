"Tomb Raider’s Notes"

AR Game design

Overview

We collaborated with the film and television IP "Tomb Raider’s Notes", utilizing the cinema space, planning and designing games based on the "Zhang Family Ancient Building" scene, the "Tomb Raider’s Notes" IP was turned into AR.

Our story, titled "The Human-Faced Turtle", takes place on the third floor of the Zhang Family Ancient Building and casts the player as the character "Wu Xie." It combines puzzle-solving and shooting game elements in an AR interactive experience, recreating the exciting plot of the original work while adding fun gameplay.

My Contribution

#1 IP analysis and product design: Participate in program design and plot planning, analyze project requirements and site characteristics, lead the writing of 3 plot program versions and 3 main characters' dubbing scripts, communicate with dubbing actors, and finalize the project's preliminary work arrangements;

#2 Main framework development: Connect with art, use Unity software, assist in the construction of the "Zhang Family Ancient Building" scene, and independently complete the development and optimization of the project's main interactive functions;

#3 Lead the project progress: Organize 4 project members to complete all content production of the project, independently complete the project report work, and assist the local TV station to complete the follow-up interview work.
